I am a PhD candidate in Combinatorics & Optimization at the University of Waterloo, advised by Chaitanya Swamy. Before joining the University of Waterloo, I completed an M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics at ETH Zurich and a dual B.Sc. in Mathematics and Physics at Universidad de los Andes.

My research interests are in Theoretical Computer Science, particularly in Combinatorial Optimization, Approximation Algorithms and Stochastic Optimization.

Together with Rian Neogi, Jacob Skitsko, and Noah Weninger, I organize the Combinatorial Optimization Reading Group at the University of Waterloo.
